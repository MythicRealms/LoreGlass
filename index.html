For Players

Your story. Your character. Your choices.

LoreGlass is a narrative RPG engine that plays like a living book. Each adventure is an authored world you explore through prose, choices, and consequences — with a full RPG system running underneath.

📖
Books You Can Lose Yourself In
Each LoreGlass book is a handcrafted story world. Prose, atmosphere, characters with memory — not menus and stats. Your character grows across books, carrying scars and gold between worlds.
⚔
A Real RPG System
Six core stats. A full equipment system with seven slots. Feats, backgrounds, statuses, and a tactical combat engine with initiative, flanking, and weapon procs. Your build matters.
💰
Trade, Barter, Survive
Gold and silver tracked in real denominations. Dynamic merchants with living economies. Bounty boards with generated jobs. Buy a rapier, sell wolf pelts, fund your next expedition.
📜
Quests That Remember
Promise Genna you'll find her son. Accept the wolf den bounty. The quest tab tracks everything — active, complete, failed. Story flags persist across sessions. The world remembers what you did.
🎲
Dice That Mean Something
Stat checks with real stakes. Roll 1d10 + Might to force the gate. Roll Cunning to bluff the guard. Success and failure both branch the story forward — no dead ends, just consequences.
🗺
Cross-Book Travel
Your character is not bound to a single story. Travel between books carrying your inventory, feats, and history. Community authors can build worlds that connect to each other.

For Authors

Write worlds. Not code.

LoreGlass ships with a full companion authoring app. Every card, quest, item, and enemy is defined in plain JSON — but you never have to write a line by hand. The Author Companion turns your imagination into a living game.

01
Card-Based Story Authoring
Write prose, set choices, chain cards together. Each card is a scene. Conditions gate choices. Effects reshape the world. The Author Companion's canvas tab shows everything at once.
02
Item & Equipment Library
Define weapons, armour, consumables and potions in a structured editor. Set slot, damage, AC bonus, sell price, and lore text. No JSON required — the engine reads it all at book load.
03
Quest & Bounty Systems
Build story quests on your bounty board with full tracking: active, complete, failed. Set keywords, descriptions, rewards. Players see them in the Quests tab the moment they accept.
04
Feats & Backgrounds
Give players build identity. Define feats with triggers (passive, active, on-hit, low HP). Create backgrounds with stat bonuses and starting gear. The character creation screen auto-populates.
05
Combat Cards
Any card can trigger a full multi-round combat with named enemies, custom stats, status-on-hit effects, and flee/victory navigation. The engine handles all the dice math.
06
Merchants & Economies
Drop a merchant card and fill structured sell/buy rows. The engine tracks merchant funds, stock, and market pricing by town. No scripting — just fill in the fields.
07
Condition & Effect System
Every choice can check conditions (keyword, stat, quest state, gold) and fire effects (give items, set flags, modify stats, start quests). Complex branching with zero code.
08
Project Audit
The Audit tab scans your whole book for broken links, orphaned cards, and keywords set but never read — or read but never set. Ship clean stories with confidence.

Development Chronicle

From First Page to Engine

v0.1 — The First Spark
Proof of Concept
  • Single scene Godot 4 prototype with hardcoded cards and choices
  • Basic prose display with choice buttons
  • First JSON card format established
v0.2 — The Book System
Dynamic Card Loading
  • JSON-driven card loading from book folders
  • Book library screen and character selection
  • First persistent keyword system — story flags that survive sessions
  • Basic save/load via .dat files
v0.3 — Characters Live
Character Creation & Stats
  • Full character creation: name, race, background
  • Six core stats (Might, Finesse, Grit, Cunning, Charisma, Lore)
  • Race presets with stat arrays and starting gold
  • HUD tabs: Stats, Items, Equipment
v0.4 — Swords and Wounds
Combat Engine
  • Turn-based combat with initiative rolls
  • Player and enemy attack resolution (d10 + stat vs AC)
  • DR damage reduction system
  • Status effects: poisoned, burning, bleeding, stunned, frozen
  • Flee, victory, and defeat navigation
v0.5 — The Market
Economy & Merchants
  • Merchant card type with structured buy/sell rows
  • Gold stored as silver integers (100s = 1g)
  • Dynamic market pricing with town-specific multipliers
  • Merchant funds, stock tracking, and cargo encumbrance
v0.6 — Notice Board
Quests & Bounties
  • Bounty board card type with generated daily jobs
  • Fixed story quest rows with accept/complete/fail keywords
  • Quest progress tracking and turn-in at merchants
  • Active quest reminder in stats HUD
v0.7 — Between Worlds
Cross-Book Travel
  • goto_book link type for cross-book navigation
  • Book state wipe on arrival, breadcrumb system
  • _prev_book and _prev_card magic values
  • Transition overlays with custom titles
  • User book folder (user://Books/) shadow-overrides built-ins
v0.8 — The Author's Desk
Author Companion App
  • Full companion authoring app (LoreGlassAuthor.gd)
  • 6 tabs: Canvas, Choices, Conditions, Effects, Encounter, Library
  • Directory + Recent card explorer with start_card pinned
  • Structured editors for combat, merchant, and bounty board cards
  • Condition and effect dropdowns with tooltips — no JSON required
  • Project Audit: broken links, orphaned cards, keyword index
  • Save All button, dirty-state tracking
v0.9 — Feats & Lore
Feats, Backgrounds, Stat Rolls
  • Feat system with passive, active, proc, and trigger types
  • Background library with stat bonuses and starting keywords

  • Combat feats: active buttons in combat, cooldown tracking

v0.95 — The Quest System
Quest Keywords & Tracking
